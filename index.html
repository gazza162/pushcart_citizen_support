Stop Mechanization, Support Doorstep Collection

For 15 years, Pune has had doorstep waste collection at ₹90/month. The Corporation now plans to replace this with a vehicle-based gate collection system.

What this means:

  1. No Doorstep Service — You must carry waste to the gate.
  2. Higher Costs — ₹90/month today vs. ₹100–₹120/month on tax bills, plus extra to take waste to the gate.
  3. More Vehicles, More Pollution — ~1,200 new diesel vehicles, more traffic in residential lanes.
  4. More Mixed Waste — Segregation fails, plants overload, risk of burning/waste-to-energy.

The Cost — ₹184 crores/year → ₹100–₹129/month per taxpayer

Citizens will pay more, get less, and face higher pollution. Less service, higher bills—raise your voice before it’s too late.
