Stop Mechanization, Support Doorstep Collection

For years, Pune’s citizens have had doorstep waste collection, with waste pickers serving bungalows, slums, flats, and societies alike. Currently, this service costs Rs. 90 per month. Now, the Corporation plans to replace it with Indore’s vehicle-based gate collection system.

What this means:

  1. No Doorstep Service — You must carry waste to the gate when the vehicle arrives.
  2. Higher Costs — ₹90/month for doorstep service today vs. ₹100–₹120/month through taxes for collection at the gate, plus your cost to bring waste to the gate.
  3. More Vehicles, More Pollution — ~1,250 new diesel vehicles, more traffic in residential lanes.
  4. More Mixed Waste — Segregation fails, plants overload, risk of burning and waste-to-energy.

The Cost — ₹184 crores/year → ₹100–₹129/month per taxpayer

Citizens will pay more, get less, and face higher pollution. Less service, higher bills—raise your voice before it’s too late.

याचा अर्थ काय:

  1. घरपोच सेवा नाही — गाडी आल्यावर तुम्हालाच कचरा गेटवर न्यायचा आहे.
  2. जास्त खर्च — आज ₹९०/महिना घरपोच सेवेसाठी; उद्या करांतून ₹१००–₹१२०/महिना फक्त गेट कलेक्शनसाठी, शिवाय गेटपर्यंत कचरा नेण्यासाठी तुमचा अतिरिक्त खर्च.
  3. अधिक गाड्या, अधिक प्रदूषण — सुमारे १,२५० नवीन डिझेल गाड्या, निवासी भागात अधिक धूर व वाहतूक कोंडी.
  4. मिश्र कचऱ्यात वाढ — वर्गीकरण थांबेल, प्रकल्पांवर ताण, जाळणी व वेस्ट-टू-एनर्जीचा धोका.
